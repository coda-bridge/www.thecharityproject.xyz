How it works

1. [Log in]: Please select your account type (Charity Staff, Charity Finance Department, Vendor, or Tutor) from the pull-down menu, then select login verification method (email, or phone no.).
· Email: Please enter your registered email, and a login link will be sent to your email. Please click that link to login.
· Phone no.: Please select +852 and enter your registered phone number, then click “Get the code”. Your WhatsApp will receive the verification code within 60 seconds. After receiving the verification code, please fill it in the box and click “Log In”. (Note: If you have not received the verification code after 60 seconds, you can click "Resend")
2. [Transaction History]: You can check out all transaction history by clicking on the transaction history button. The history record will be filtered by month. The transaction’s counterparty, time, and amount, or hour, will be recorded. To look into transactions happening in previous months, click on the down arrow to switch.
3. [Scan In]: For the Charity Staff account, scan the Tutor’s QR code to record the starting time of the service.
4. [Scan QR Code]: For the Charity Staff account, scan the Tutor’s QR code again to record the ending time of the service, and the staff needs to enter the hour that the tutor provided.
5. [Convert to Fiat]: For the Vendor account, convert the remaining balance of the Coda tokens to fiat money.
